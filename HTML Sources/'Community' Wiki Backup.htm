    Version: 2.0.0
    ----------------------------------------------------------------
    Navigation and General Hotkeys:
    Keyboard:
    F8: Open and Close the UI
    NumPad 8: Scroll Upwards
    NumPad 2: Scroll Downwards
    NumPad 4: Scroll Left
    NumPad 6: Scroll Right
    NumPad 5: Select Current Option/Open Category
    NumPad 0: Go Back/Close Category
    NumPad +: Fire Vehicle Rockets (if enabled under Vehicle Options)
    F5: Clean Player/Refill Health
    F6 + M: Teleport to Mission Objective
    F6 + C: Teleport to Crate
    F7: Teleport to Waypoint
    F9: Clear Forced Animations (For when skiddos put you in an animation loop)
    J - Breathe Fire (if enabled under Weapon Options)

    Xbox and PlayStation Controllers:
    (PlayStation-equivalent controls are marked in parenthesis)
    Right Bumper + DPad Left (R1 + DPad Left): Open and Close the UI
    DPad Up: Scroll Upwards
    DPad Down: Scroll Downwards
    DPad Left: Scroll Left
    DPad Right: Scroll Right
    A (X): Select Current Option/Open Category
    B (O): Go Back/Close Category
    Left Analogue Stick: Fire Vehicle Rockets (if enabled under Vehicle Options)
