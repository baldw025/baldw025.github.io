    Offset Y - This allows you to modify the Community2 UI's position in respect to its y-axis.
    Display Info Message At Top - When enabled, Community2 server notifications will be displayed at the top left of your screen whenever they're available.
    Gradients - When enabled, fancy UI gradients will be used.
    Titlebox Globe - When enabled, the globe located in the primary header will be shown. It's a pretty nice globe.
    Centre Title - When enabled, the header text will always be centered.
    Centre Breaks - When enabled, breaks (---This is a break---) will always be centered.
    Startup (Vehicle) Ram - When enabled, you'll be kissed by a T20 upon initialization of Community2.
    Sync Community2 With Config File - When enabled, each option you enable, disable, modify, and etcetera, will immediately be saved to your "Community2Config" file in your "Community2Stuff" folder.
    Friendly Note: A practice I recommend with this sync setting is to enable all your essential settings, like no ragdoll, seatbelt, show who's talking, infinite ammo, and etcetera, then turning the sync off. I recommend this practice as I find it annoying to find out you accidentally have god mode or some other weird option on when you play GTA the next day. Can also keep you "safe" in some situations

    Reset Toggles (Most Of Them)     - When selected, most of your menu toggles will be reset to their default setting. Use this if you can't troubleshoot which option is making you crash or whatever.
